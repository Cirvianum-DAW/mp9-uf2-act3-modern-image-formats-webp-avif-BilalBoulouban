What is the most optimal format for this type of image and why? If the image requires transparency, PNG is the most optimal format because it supports transparency, while JPEG does not. What happens when we save it in JPEG format? If you save a transparent image in JPEG format, the transparent areas will become white or another solid color, as JPEG does not support transparency. If our website has a white background, what format would be the most optimal and why? If your website has a white background and the image does not require transparency, JPEG could be more optimal due to its better compression capabilities. However, if the image requires transparency, PNG would still be the best choice.
