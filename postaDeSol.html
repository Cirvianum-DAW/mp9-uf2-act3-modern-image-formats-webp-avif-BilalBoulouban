What format is the most optimal for this type of image and why? For a sunset image, JPEG is usually the most optimal because it provides good quality for complex images and has better compression than PNG. With what compression quality does the image not lose quality? For JPEG, a compression level of around 70-80% usually maintains a good balance between size and quality. Can we reduce its size even more without losing quality? You can reduce the size further with optimization tools, but extreme compression may reduce quality. What would happen if we save the image several times with JPEG format? If you save an image multiple times in JPEG format, it will undergo 'generation loss', as JPEG is a lossy format. Each save reduces the quality of the image.
